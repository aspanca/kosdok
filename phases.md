## Phase 1 – Foundation MVP

### Patient Side:

- Search doctors by specialty & location
- View doctor/clinic profiles
- Book appointments
- Appointment history

### Clinic Dashboard:

- Manage calendar & availability
- Confirm/cancel bookings
- Basic patient list
- SMS/email reminders

- Personal medical timeline
- View test results -> uploaded from clincks
- Compare during the year

### Clinic:

- Add visit notes
- Upload lab results
- Attach prescriptions
- Role-based access (doctor/admin/lab)
